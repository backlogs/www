The known-good platform is Redmine 2.0.4 on either ruby 1.8.7 or 1.9.3, on Linux, with a from-source (i.e., not a platform-packaged) install. Windows and pre-packaged distros such as Bitnami ought to work in principle, but are untested, and history shows that getting them to work requires a bit of effort on your account.

It would in principle be possible to support Redmine 1.4.4 and 2.1.2, and ChiliProject v3.3.0. Much of the plumbing is in place, but these platforms need someone to adopt them. You'd get our help, but they're currently known not to work, and no directed effort is being spent right now to remedy that.

Please remember that this is a volunteer effort and our time is limited; unless you are on the latest released version (currently {% include version.html %}) of the plugin on one of the supported versions above we will probably still help you but it's not a priority, nor an obligation. Sorry, but those are the breaks.
